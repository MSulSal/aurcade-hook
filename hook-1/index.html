Why this hooks

Built Around Aurcade's Real Flywheel

1. Player Loop

Quick score entry, instant ranking, and local bragging rights keep players returning to the same machine.

2. Venue Loop

Operators get fresh weekly events and machine-specific traffic without adding staffing overhead.

3. Community Loop

Public leaderboards, venue pages, and social-ready moments create shareable content every day.

4. Growth Loop

Every high score becomes a discovery point that attracts both players and new partner venues.

Experience Direction

Retro Soul, Modern Funnel

Arcade-native visuals

Pixel fonts, scanline texture, cabinet-inspired framing, and high contrast score tables.

Operator-friendly UX

Clean task paths, fast loading layouts, and practical controls for promotions, events, and score moderation.

Mobile-first participation

Players should join contests, claim scores, and share highlights in seconds from their phones.
